About

Designing things
that mean something.

Mumbai · Available remotely worldwide

Hi, I'm Aria. I've spent the last decade making things — first at a small studio in Bombay, then briefly in-house at a magazine, and for the past six years on my own.

I work in close partnership with founders and editors who care deeply about how their work looks and reads. Most engagements run six to twelve weeks. I take on roughly eight clients a year, with at least one personal or pro-bono project mixed in.

If you're building something with a strong point of view and you want a collaborator rather than a vendor, I'd love to hear from you.
