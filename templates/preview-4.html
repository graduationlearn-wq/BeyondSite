Our Foundation

We didn't build Helix.
to be another platform.
We built it to be the last one you need.

Helix Protocol was founded in 2021 by two infrastructure engineers — one from Coinbase Custody, one from Polygon — who watched the on-chain ecosystem fragment into incompatible silos and decided to build the connective tissue that should have existed all along.

Our architecture is non-custodial by design, our contracts are audited by Trail of Bits and OpenZeppelin, and our infrastructure is monitored on-chain around the clock. We do not ask you to trust us — we make trust unnecessary. Every contract is open-source, every upgrade is timelocked, every parameter is publicly governed.

Every team that builds on Helix gets direct access to protocol engineers, not a support ticket queue. That is the difference between a vendor and a partner — and that is the only kind of company we know how to be.
