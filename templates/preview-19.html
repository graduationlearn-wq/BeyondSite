Keep these handy

A short, predictable document list.

We make sure your application is complete the first time. Below is the standard set most lenders ask for — plus what changes if you are self-employed or applying for a home loan.

  • PAN card · Aadhaar card
  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Last 6 months bank statement
  • Latest Form 16 OR last 2 years ITR
  • Address proof (Aadhaar / utility / rent agreement)
  • PAN card · Aadhaar card
  • Last 3 years ITR + computation
  • Last 12 months bank statement (current + savings)
  • Business proof (GST/Udyam/incorporation)
  • Profit & Loss + Balance Sheet (CA-certified for ₹25L+)
  • Property documents — sale agreement, title deed
  • Property tax receipts (last 3 years)
  • NOC from society / builder
  • Approved building plan
  • For purchase: allotment letter + cost sheet

What you should know

The bits banks bury in fine print.

Mandatory disclosures every customer should read before applying. We surface these up-front rather than in the consent screen of a 14-page form.

💯
You pay us nothing
Our commission is paid by the lender on successful disbursal. DhanSetu Loans never charges the customer.
⚖
Lender has final say
Loan approval is at the lender sole discretion. Rates and amounts on this site are indicative — your actual offer depends on credit appraisal.
💸
Lender charges still apply
Processing fees, GST, stamp duty, prepayment charges, and other levies are collected by the lender per their published schedule.
📜
Documents stay with lender
KYC, income, and CKYC records you submit are held by the lender per RBI norms. We do not retain originals.
🛡
DPDP Act 2023 compliant
Your personal data is processed only for the loan you are seeking. Right to access, correct, or delete on written request.
🚪
No guaranteed approvals
We do not guarantee any loan. Beware of anyone — DSA or otherwise — claiming guaranteed approval in exchange for an upfront fee.
