Attention Investors

1. Prevent unauthorised transactions in your account — update your mobile numbers and email IDs with your stock broker and depository participant. Receive transaction alerts from the Exchange and CDSL/NSDL directly.

2. KYC is a one-time exercise — once KYC is done through a SEBI-registered intermediary, you need not undergo the process again when approaching another intermediary.

3. For IPO applications, there is no need to issue a cheque — sign the IPO application form to authorise your bank to make payment in case of allotment. In case of non-allotment, funds remain in your bank account.

4. Pay 20% upfront margin of the transaction value to trade in the cash market segment. Investors may refer to the Exchange's frequently issued FAQs and guidelines.

5. Check your securities, mutual funds, and bonds in the consolidated account statement issued by NSDL/CDSL every month.
